Welcome

Welcome to the CAICI Research Group led by Dr. Xi Hu in the Department of Technology at Illinois State University!

Mission

Our core mission is to advance and enrich the managerial practices of built assets (e.g., buildings, bridges, and pavements), construction workforce, and civil infrastructures through domain knowledge-enhanced, data-driven, technology-enabled, and AI-powered solutions. Our team focuses on developing automated and intelligent systems empowered by emerging technologies like artificial intelligence, robotics, and digital twins to address critical issues in construction and infrastructure management sector, such as aging and deteriorating infrastructures, and construction labor shortage.

About Us

This is a newly established research group based in the Department of Technology, College of Applied Science and Technology, at Illinois State University. Our goal is to create safer, more efficient, and resilient built environment by interfacing cutting-edge digital tools with civil engineering practice.
