Welcome

Welcome to the iCANdu Research Group based in the Department of Technology at Illinois State University! Our core mission is to develop intelligent and advanced systems with emerging technologies like artificail intelligence (AI) and digital twins to advance and enrich the managerial practices of built assets (e.g., buildings, and bridges), construction workforce, and civil infrastructures through domain knowledge-enhanced, data-driven, technology-enabled, and AI-powered solutions. Our primary researcg interests include construction automation, intelligent infrastructure systems, applied robotics, smart sensing, and advanced data analytics.
